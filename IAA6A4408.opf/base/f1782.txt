atha khalu bhagavān punar evāyuṣmaṃtaṃ mahākāśyapam āmaṃtrayati sma · yasmiṃ kāśyapa deśe uṣṭradhūmaka kṛṣṇaśira uttānaśā
yī bhavati sa deśa sopadravaḥ sopakleśa sopāyāso bhavati · sacet puna kāśyapa yasmiṃ deśe bodhisatvo bhavati · sa deśa n
irupadrava nirupakleśa nirupāyāso bhavati · tasmāt tarhi kāśyapa satvārthodyuktena bodhisatvena bhavitavyaṃ tena sarvaku
śalamūlāni sarvasatvānām utsraṣṭavyaṃ · sarvaṃ ca kuśalamūla samyak samudānayitavyaṃ · yac ca jñānabheṣajyaṃ paryeṣate t
ena caturdiśaṃ gatvā sarvasatvānāṃ bhūtacikitsā kartavyā : bhūtacikitsāyāc ca satvā cikitsitavyāḥ
