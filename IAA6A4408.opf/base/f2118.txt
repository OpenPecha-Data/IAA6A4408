ārī caturbhi īryāpathair lūhānnapānabhojīsaṃtuṣṭaḥ caturbhir āryavaṃśer asaṃsṛṣṭo gṛhasthapravrajitair alpabhāṣyo ’lpama
ṃtraḥ te cāsyeryāpathāḥ kuhanalapanatayā kalpitā bhavaṃti · na cittapariśuddhaye · na śamāya nopaśamāya · na damāya · up
alaṃbhadṛṣṭikaś ca bhavati · śunyatānupalaṃbhāś ca dharmeṣu śrrutvā prapātasaṃjñī bhavati · śunyatāvādināṃ ca bhikṣuṇām
